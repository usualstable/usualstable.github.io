Cookie Policy


At Crypto Pulse Journal, we are committed to protecting your privacy and providing you with the best experience while using our website. To enhance website functionality and analyze user interactions, we use cookies.

What are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us recognize your device on subsequent visits and can be used for personalization, traffic analysis, improving website performance, and ensuring necessary website features function correctly.

How We Use Cookies
We use the following types of cookies on our site:

Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for the operation of the website. They allow you to navigate the site and use essential features like accessing secure areas or customizing preferences.

Analytical Cookies: These cookies help us track and analyze website traffic, providing valuable insights to improve user experience and optimize content.

Functional Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences (like language settings) to enhance your experience when using the site.

Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to display personalized ads and measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

Managing Cookies
You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. However, blocking certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

For more information on managing cookies in your browser, please visit your browser’s help section or support page.
